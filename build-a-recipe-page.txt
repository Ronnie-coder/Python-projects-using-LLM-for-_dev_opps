<h1>Chocolate Chip Cookies</h1>
<p>This is a delicious and easy-to-make recipe that you'll love!</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
     <li>1 cup of flour</li>
     <li>2 eggs</li>
     <li>1/2 cup of sugar</li>
     <li>1 teaspoon of vanilla extract</li>
</ul>
<h2>Instructions</h2>
<ol>
     <li>reheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).</li>
     <li>mix flower and sugar in a bowl.</li>
    <li>Add eggs and vanilla extract,mix well</li>
    <li>Pour butter in the greased baking pan</li>
    <li>Bake for 30 minutes or untill golden brown</li>
</ol>
